⚡ Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built specifically for university competition management — every feature serves a real purpose.

📋

Streamlined Submissions

Submit competitions with all required details in one structured form. No emails, no lost documents.

✅

Multi-level Approval

Two-stage review by PIC and Faculty Admin with full notes and rejection reasons kept on record.

📄

Auto Exemption Letters

Official exemption letters are generated automatically upon faculty approval — ready to download as PDF.

📈

Analytics & KPIs

Real-time dashboards showing submission rates, funding totals, achievement stats per major.

🏅

Achievement Tracking

Record competition outcomes — ranks, medals, and certificates — building a full achievement history.

🔍

Full Audit Log

Every login, approval, rejection, and export is logged with timestamp. Super Admins see everything.
